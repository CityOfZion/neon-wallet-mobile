# Translation Glossary

Project-specific terms and translation rules for Neon Wallet Desktop.

## pt-br

| Term | Translation / Rule |
|------|--------------------|
| Neon (the wallet) | Always feminine: "a Neon", "da Neon", "na Neon" — implicitly refers to "a carteira Neon" |
| blockchain | Feminine: "a blockchain" — follows "cadeia de blocos" |
